Salbutamol 4mg

Salbutamol(Cipla) 4 mg Tablet is a bronchodilator medicine that is 
used to relieve the symptoms of bronchospasm such as wheezing, 
shortness of breath, chest tightness, breathing difficulties, 
coughing, etc., in respiratory diseases like bronchial asthma 
and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD
